What we're known for

کھوئے والے مٹن چنے

Sufi G has been dishing out Wazirabad's favourite khoya-style mutton chanay for years — rich, slow-cooked chickpeas with tender mutton, finished with the smoky khoya that gives the dish its name. Come for the chanay, stay for the paye, haleem and halwa puri.
